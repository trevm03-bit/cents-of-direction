Questions, answered plainly

Will it actually look good printed?

Yes — every tab was designed against a white page from the start, with muted accent colors chosen for AA-level contrast and toner economy, not just "readable enough." Print any tab straight from Google Sheets.

Is this a PDF or a Google Sheet?

A live Google Sheet, not a PDF. After checkout you'll get a link to click "Make a Copy" — it lands in your own Drive, fully editable, no plugin or login beyond your normal Google account.

Do I have to pick a method right away?

No — What-If Lab always shows Snowball vs. Avalanche vs. Credit Score Focus side by side for your real numbers, so you can compare before committing, and change your mind later without losing anything.

Can I see all four methods compared everywhere, or just on one tab?

The What-If Lab tab is exactly where that happens — it automatically compares Snowball, Avalanche, and Credit Score Focus side by side, using your real numbers. Every other tab (My Plan, Dashboard, Payment Schedule, Track It, Progress) follows whichever single method you've chosen on My Plan, and "Your own order" is a 4th option you can select there too — it just sits outside the automatic 3-way comparison since it's a custom ranking, not a fixed formula.

Is this financial advice?

No. This tool offers general organization and planning structure, not financial or legal advice. For decisions specific to you, talk to a licensed professional.
